MY PLAN

      As a "teen", I have decided to design a simple website for fun. As I continue to do so, it provokes me to become a full stack web developer in my future years. With the constant advent of newer technology, I believe that soon almost all of things we do in America will be based on technology. Sadly, not everywhere has the oppurtunity God has given me. And I take the responsibilty to reach everybody in the world and allow them to have a taste of technology.
      My goals, however, require perserverance after the various obstacles I encounter. I believe that in order to succeed, you must overcome. However, to overcome you must have determination; and when you do not what like what you are doing, you will never have the true determination. And after all, if you love what you do you will become a master because the only way to become a master is to love what you do. When you become a master, you will be paid a good fee. So the question is, "What do you want to be?"
