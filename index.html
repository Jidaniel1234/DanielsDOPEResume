MY PAST

Over the last couple years I have probably been more productive than the rest of my life. Beginning in 4th grade, I was invited by my school to represent our team in the MathCount's field day event in our region, recieving a 3rd place and having a memorable time. Also, I began basketball in my city's team. Starting in 5th grade, I started enrolling into after school math classes, and learned Algebra that year. Continuing on into 6th grade, I entered the MathCounts team there to but unable to compete since there were 8th graders that were much more experienced than me. In 7th grade, I started to learn how to code, learning some HTML and CSS as well as rejoin the team and compete in regionals, recieving a 3rd place again. Additionally, our school's Science Olympiad was interesting and decided to join that as well. So here I am now, in 8th grade, creating my first functional (made others before but were pretty cringy) website, in Science Olympiad and MathCounts again, and taking Pre-Calculus.
